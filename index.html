Sustainable City Planning helps urban planners make data-driven decisions in the face of climate change. Users can place markers representing developments to gain insight into local and global climate impacts. This tool promotes urban expansion, sustainability, and responsible decision making.


Drop a pin on the interactive web map to dynamically explore the impacts of developments such as factories or infrastructure on the selected region. View summaries of the potential effects of city growth on places that matter to you!



Sustainable City Considerations

Transportation

Prioritizing public transportation, cycling, and walking over private car ownership.

Water Conservation

Urban water management through awareness and responsible treatment/storage/distribution.

Waste Management

Developing programs that promote reducing, reusing, recycling, and composting.

Green Spaces

Incorporating community gardens, parks, and green roofs into cities.

Urban Renewal

Improving existing infrastructure in favor of new construction.
